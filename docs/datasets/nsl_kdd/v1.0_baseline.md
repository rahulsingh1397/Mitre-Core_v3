## Known Limitations

1. **Single checkpoint** — results are tied to `network_v9_v3/network_it_best.pt`. New checkpoints require a new version.
2. **Single label track as headline** — `tactic` collapses dissimilar attacks; `campaign_id` may be a more valid track. Re-evaluation with a primary on `campaign_id` would be v1.1.
3. **Phase 3 sweep methodology debt** — a standalone HDBSCAN sweep was run during development and showed catastrophic ARI degradation when ported to the full engine. The current config was hand-selected and validated, not grid-searched through the full engine. `benchmark/clustering_sweep_full_engine.py` exists as the correct future sweep path.
4. **No heavy GNN baselines yet** — HGT, HAN, R-GCN, MAGIC, FLASH not included. When added, this becomes v1.1 with a retro comparison.
5. **CPU-only latency** — 7.9 s/10k measured on CPU; GPU path was not exercised (peak_gpu_gb = 0.0).
