## ⚠️ Known Sensitivity — Knife-Edge PCA Optimum

The v1.0 → v1.1 improvement is the result of a **sharp non-monotone spike at pca=11**:

| `hdbscan_pca_components` | V3 ARI |
|---|---|
| 8 | 0.357 |
| 11 | **0.461** ⬅ v1.1 |
| 12 | 0.381 |
| 13 | 0.359 |
| 16 (v1.0) | 0.355 |
| 20 | 0.353 |

The +0.106 lift comes from a single-component sweet spot. ARI returns to ~0.36 at pca=12 (one above) and pca=10/13. This is fully deterministic across seeds 42/43/44 (std=0.0000), so it's not noise — but it's a knife-edge result. Future codebase changes (PyTorch version, HDBSCAN version, embedding-encoder weight reload) could displace the optimum, and v1.1 reproducibility tests are the early-warning system. If the v1.1 reproduction test ever fails post-update, treat this as an investigative signal and re-sweep pca ∈ {9,10,11,12,13} before assuming a regression.
